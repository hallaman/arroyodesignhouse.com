Cedar Forks Blanket Ladder

Blanket ladders are a staple in our home -- they are both practical for storage and perfect for displaying our favorite blankets. These ladders are made from cedar and are left unfinished to allow the natural properties of the wood to do what it does best -- smell amazing and ward off moths. The ladder is 68.5" x 23", tapering to 16" at the top. There are five rungs to accommodate five blankets. The standard ladder does not include a cut-out, but designs can be special ordered.
